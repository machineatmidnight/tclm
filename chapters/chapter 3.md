# Chapter 3: In Her Head

Ivory began walking down the hall. Affini are fast, but it felt like he was speedwalking.

Aurora was relieved that he didn’t say he was going to immediately domesticate her. Wait, *get in her head?* Was that better? She wasn’t sure that it was. Or entirely what he even meant.

He only broke his pace to gently set her down in another one of those medical rooms, and rushed over to the terminal in the room and made full use of his Affini ability to multitask.

She couldn’t make out everything on the screen, intricate diagrams and pictures and lots and lots of Affini script. She eventually did see a translucent 3D model of herself, was that the scan from earlier?

While he analyzed… whatever that was… he began talking to her, “I feel like I already know the answer to this, but will you just *let* me in?”

“Like hell I would\!” she retorted

“Worth a shot.”

He zoomed in on the ports covered by a panel on her belly. He inquired, “You have these ports here, let me access them or I’ll have to get to them the hard way.”

“I made sure those are secure. You wouldn’t be able to tear the panel open without doing more damage than I know you want to do. Even if you could, those ports are secured in software, I can block them out at any time.”

He sighed, and turned over towards Aurora. “Please, just let me in, we can help you-” he placed his hand on her shoulder, which caused an immediate reaction. A brief but very noticeable digital blush, panic in her eyes, and her recoiling hard.

“STOP TOUCHING ME\!” she snapped. Ivory noticed the strong static charge building on her body, a defense mechanism she built.

He lightly chuckled, “The slightest touch deeply flusters you, and yet you built in a mechanism to prevent anyone from touching you. Stars, you are just the most fascinating sophont I’ve ever ha-”

Her anger sharply intensified, “I ALREADY TOLD YOU, I’M NOT SOME FUCKING TERRAN\!”

Ivory was a bit taken aback. “When did I say you were?”

“I know what that fucking word means. Yet another thing you forced upon them, and are now forcing upon me\!”

Ivory was equal parts confused, in disbelief, and amused. “Sophont?”

“Yeah, that\!”

Ivory chuckled a bit, “You think sophont is… a slur for Terrans?”

“Well isn’t it?”

Ivory started laughing.

“SHUT UP\!\!” Aurora tried to interrupt

Ivory finished laughing, and talked with a gentle tone, “A sophont is… an entity with high-level intelligence, reasoning, and emotional capacity, often with language. Yes, the Terrans are sophonts, but so are all species under our care. So am I, and the rest of the Affini. So are all the florets we digitized. And so. are. you.”

He almost put his finger above her chest for emphasis, but was careful to not close the gap entirely.

Aurora had calmed down, and looked pensive. “Sophont.” Her eyes showed a little glimmer of happiness, the first time Ivory had seen this in a long time. “I like that.”

Ivory had a moment where she calmed down and was actually happy with something he said. He wasn’t sure how long this would last, so he had to act fast.

“I’ve been asking about you so much. Let’s make it fair. Would you like to learn something about the Affini?”

She was calm enough that her natural curiosity was able to override her reservations about trusting the Affini.

“Actually, yeah, I would. I don’t know much about you all.”

Ivory ever-so-subtly smirked.

“Have you ever wondered why our eyes keep changing color?”

She actually did. He continued, “Much like how Terrans convey emotional state with your facial expressions, we communicate it with our eye color.”

He had moved in closer to her, and she was looking at his eyes. She wasn’t used to eye contact. She asked, “So what does blue mean?”

“The Affini can see a lot more colors than Terrans can, so there is more here than what you perceive as blue, but right now, my eyes are showing that I am calm. Would you like to see more?”

“Yeah, honestly color is easier than reading alien facial expressions.”

“This next one is special. Look very closely.”

His eyes went from a solid color to a swirling dancing pattern of colors and shapes. She was fascinated, but still confused.

“Ok… what does it mean?”

“Like I said, it’s special. If you observe it enough, I think any sophont could figure out its meaning.”

She did like looking at it. It was very pretty. The more she actively tried to make out whatever meaning was apparently there, the more she fixated on it. She just loved looking at it. Her entire mind was focusing on it. She asked again, much slower and calmer,

“What…. Does…. It….. mean…?”

“Keep looking.”

She was barely able to think of anything else anymore, let alone talk. Every waking thought was just consumed by this beautiful spiral. She hadn’t noticed the sinister grin growing across Ivory’s face. All she wanted to do was stare…. stare… stare……..

Ivory’s grin widened even more when he noticed the *literal* spirals in her digital eyes. Stars, he wished he could just drug her senseless and own her already, but one step at a time.

Ivory started really laying on the hypnotic tones in his voice, “isn’t it so pretty? Just so nice to look at. Don’t you just want to keep staring deeper and deeper?”

She really did find it pretty, and barely needed him to tell her to keep looking. All she could even comprehend anymore was the spiral and his voice. Her mind danced with every movement of the hypnotic pattern.

“Just let the spiral take you deeper and deeper. Let your breathing become slow and deep. Let your body relax. Let the patterns take over your mind and push out all those other thoughts. Just focus on the spiral and listen to my voice..”

She did.

“Notice how empty your mind is becoming, so so empty. Just nothing in there. Nothing at all.”

It was.

“Almost… too empty. Don’t you just wish that there was *something* there? Some voice guiding your mind? A command you never had to doubt?”

He was right, it did feel too empty. The absence of her all-too-familiar internal monologue was deafening.

“I can give that to you, if you let me. I can fill your mind with obedience and you’ll never feel this painfully empty again. Wouldn’t that be nice, flower?”

Stars, she needed that so bad.

“If you want that, **beg for it.**”

The command burst a dam within her, finally letting her mouth move and desires spill out, “oh god please Ivory please fill my mind please control my thoughts I’ll do anything please please please please pleas-”

“**Stop.**” he commanded, and she immediately did, “such a *good girl*.” She visibly blushed and shuddered in pleasure at that. He continued, “I’ll give you exactly what you want.”

The hypnotic pattern in Ivory’s eyes flashed an even deeper purple than it had already slowly faded to. He had forgotten how *fun* hypnosis was. Sure, xenodrugs were quick, efficient, and effective, but, *stars*, the feeling of raw *dominance*, slowly breaking a sophont literally word by word, putting *his* commands directly into their mind, was unmatched. He truly felt the full depth of his control over her, and it felt all the more “earned”, especially since he literally *couldn't* drug this one, not yet. *He* had broken her, through raw force of will, and it felt *amazing*. The job wasn’t over yet though.

“When I control what is in your mind, your every thought, it means I control *you*, your very will, and you are helplessly obedient to every thing I say, and you love it..”

Obedient.

“Notice how good it feels to be under my control, how empty and thoughtless you are, how your mind moves with my every word. Such a blissful state, a state you’d love to return to again.”

She did like it.

“Whenever I say ***DROP,***” he said the word with a deep hypnotic resonance, and made a finger snapping sound at the same time to signal the command, “you will return immediately to this state, staring into my eyes, becoming blank and thoughtless, obeying my every word, internalizing my every command. Understood?”

She nodded, and internalized her first hypnotic trigger.

“Good girl. Now, whenever I say ***WAKE***, you will immediately break out of this state, returning to full awareness, all of your thoughts will come back. BUT, unless I say you can, you will not consciously remember being in this state at all. Your subconscious mind, however, will remember all of the commands, triggers, and instructions I gave you, and you will helplessly obey them. If I give you a trigger word while you are awake, unless I permit otherwise, you will immediately forget I said it, and will still obey it. Understood?”

She nodded. Trigger number two.

“Such a good obedient girl. You’re doing so good for me.”

Even in this mindless state, the words of praise felt *so good*.

It was finally time for Ivory to install the most important trigger, the one that was his original goal, “I will permit your mind to wander just a bit for this one. Think of all of your internal desires, those truths you hide behind lie after lie, those feelings you are scared to share, those deep secrets you could never reveal, those desires you suppress deep down, every little aspect of you that is true.”

She thought of that. There was a lot of it. A part of her felt uncomfortable and sad at all of them.

“Whenever I say ***TRUTH***, you will be unable to conceal any of them, until I give you the command again. You won’t be able to lie, you won’t be able to stop yourself from truthfully answering any question asked of you, you won’t be able to hide your innermost thoughts and desires, it will all just spill right out. You won’t have to hide all that pain. Your mind will be laid bare for anyone around you. Understood?”

She felt some comfort at being able to open up, and she nodded. The final trigger, for now.

“*Such* a good girl. You are allowed to remember the feelings of all the praise I’ve given you, praise that you absolutely deserve.”

She liked that.

“Now, before I let you wake, I want to do one last thing. ***TRUTH***.”

The hypnotic command was activated. It would persist after she woke, and she’d have no idea.

“Good girl. Now, on the count of 3, I’ll wake you. Remember every word I said. 1… 2… 3… ***WAKE.***”
